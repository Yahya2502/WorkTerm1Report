Namaa Infologistics

Company

Cyber Science Lab

Company


The Cyber Science Lab (CSL) is an academic research lab operating within the Canada Cyber Foundry (CCF) at the School of Computer Science, University of Guelph in Ontario, Canada. Its primary focus is advancing the field of autonomous cybersecurity, with a special interest in the development of autonomous incident detection and response systems, autonomous security of AI systems, autonomous protection of critical infrastructure, and autonomous offensive security.


Interesting facts about CSL


  • Dr. Ali Dehghantanha is the founder and director of the Cyber Science Lab.

  • The Lab was established in August 2018.

  • TheCyber Science Lab Podcast showcases groundbreaking research in cybersecurity, featuring esteemed guests and innovative initiatives.

  • The lab will host the 1st International Workshop on Autonomous Cybersecurity (AutonomousCyber 2024) in conjunction with ACM CCS 2024, happening in Salt Lake City, U.S.A., on October 18, 2024.
